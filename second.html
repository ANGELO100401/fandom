Legendary Players of Los Angeles Lakers

Magic Johnson

Widely regarded as one of the greatest point guards in NBA history. He won five NBA championships with the Lakers and was a 12-time NBA All-Star.

Kobe Bryant

Considered one of the greatest basketball players of all time. He won five NBA championships with the Lakers and was an 18-time NBA All-Star.

Kareem Abdul-Jabbar

Widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time. He won five NBA championships with the Lakers and was a 19-time NBA All-Star.
